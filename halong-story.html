3 Days On Halong Bay Magazine Spread

A non-conventional grid and overlapping script typography adds to a visual sense of adventure, along with elements that invoke a sense of place and movement. Colors are warm, vibrant and harmonious with the image, chosen for its visceral representation of the subject of the article. Body fonts are legible but expressive, adding a final element of character to the piece.

Photography by myself, and body copy is from Aileen Adalid's excellent travel blog, iamaileen.com.
